It does not get any better on the original Nintendo. Zelda II: The Adventure of Link was a game that provided it all on this original console. From a top down view, to a side scroller, to maps galore, puzzles, difficult if not near-impossible boss fights at times. This game spawned a series that would continue for the next 30+ years. And that gold cartridge... When this game was pulled out of its sleeve, it truly did feel like an adventure.

Tecmo Super Bowl had it all: all teams, an interchangeable roster, a full season, all the stats you can imagine, and Bo Jackson! Overnight stays were common when friends came together to play out an entire season and face off against each other in the Super Bowl. This game truly introduced millions of kids to the game of football, and in their later years of adulthood, a Madden Football addiction.

Click on a Game to Display the Dialog Box

R.C. Pro-Am was a racing game on the original Nintendo. Unlike prior racing games like Pole Position on the Atari 2600, and numerous copycat games that followed, RC ProAm gave the player 32 tracks, power-ups and a true challenge. It can be argued that this game laid the foundation to the massively popular racing series, Mario Kart. What it does not get credit for is the thirst it caused for more racing; which other popular racing series like Gran Turismo and Forza Racing have benefitted from in the older gamers who first started with RC ProAm on the original Nintendo.
